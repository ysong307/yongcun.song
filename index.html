About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Example. My research interests lie in the intersection of partial differential equations and mathematical physics, with a focus on nonlinear waves and integrable systems.

I received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from Example University in 2018, under the supervision of Professor Jane Smith. Before joining University of Example, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Example Institute of Technology.
